## Panel Notes

For each panel:

- What changed?
- Where should the viewer look first?
- What is the character thinking or trying to do?
- Is the silhouette readable?
- What should be held, cut, or emphasized?

## Animatic Checks

- Does the story work without extra explanation?
- Is the main action readable in every shot?
- Are the holds long enough for decisions and reveals?
- Does the final beat land clearly?
